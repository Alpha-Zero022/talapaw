Karen Traditional Talapaw Soup

Recipe

  1. Boil the bamboo shoots with two litters of water and wait for twelve minutes until all the bitter taste inside the bamboo shoots is eliminated.
  2. Add one cap of grinded rice and wait for two minutes.
  3. In this step you may add grinded pepper, shrimp, grinded dried shrimp, and ladyfinger, and flavor as you want.
  4. Finally, you may add basil. Now you can enjoy the most delicious Karen traditional soup called "Talapaw".
